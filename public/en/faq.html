FAQ

  • What is Quorum ?

    Quorum is a mobilisation technology startup.
    We build an app that change the way of volunteering making it more accessible for people & efficient for organisations, using the latest techs in data and artificial intelligence.

  • Why use Quorum ?

    Face to face and person to person is the most effective way to engage someone.
    Knowing this, as an organisation, a campaign, a movement, an administration, a company or a game changer you have to be able to :

    - organise efficiently and empower your team to mobilise widely
    - reduce efforts given by volunteers by using dedicated tools and by targeting areas and messages.

    Use Quorum, to help you to share your vision thanks to a powerful organisation.

  • What devices does Quorum work on ?

    Our app is cross-platform. It works with Apple IOS devices, Android devices and also tablets. The web app can be used with any personal computer with an Internet connection.

  • Is Quorum just a software ?

    Yes and No
    Yes, because we are not a consulting firm or your staff director or your COLLECTIVE etc.

    We are a tech provider, offering powerful tools for your team and meaningful analysis for your organisation to take better decisions.

    Those analyse can helps you out but the decision and the strategy remains yours. Your data, Your decisions.

    NO, because we are not just a software but also a service. We make our client a top priority because we care about their success. We have a team dedicated, making sure that we provide trainings, feedbacks, implementation, best practices and support. For any question contact Max

  • I am only interested with one feature, and not the others …

    Not a problem. You can use one of our feature without the other. It depends on your needs and the momentum of your action.

  • Does Quorum respect privacy ?

    Yes, this is important to us. Privacy, Intimity and civil liberties are fundamental rights. More than a concern, at Quorum it is a value.
    Even more importantly we have to be mission driven, and be meaningful in our work.

    In many cases we don’t need personal data to make our job done.

    If data creates exciting opportunities, we have to look at those opportunities without forgetting the outcome we are targeting and without forgetting our values. We built the company to solve people’s problems, and bring positive change ; not to create new ones.

    Ours apps use "privacy by design" principles and we have a legal expert dedicated on the team. You can contact him if you have any question or inquiries.

    Modern apps. Full Privacy.
