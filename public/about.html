What We Believe In

Data & technology don’t build movements or share passions, people do.

This is why we’ve created Quorum; to create positive social change and empower people though technology. But we need to look at the future and these news goals & opportunities without forgetting our core values.

  • PRIVACY & CIVIL LIBERTIES
  • EMANCIPATE DATA
  • OPEN SOFWARE
  • ACHIEVE OUTCOMES AND GET REAL IMPACT
  • TECHNOLOGY HAVE TO ADAPT TO HUMAN, NOT THE OTHER WAY AROUND

Because technology and data can’t be the ultimate goal; it is just a mean to help PEOPLE and organization to optimize their everyday actions and achieve goals.
